---
title: "Create a new check package"
---

If your compliance needs change, you have the flexibility to create new check packages tailored to your specific requirements.

1. Log in to your [HirePass](https://workspace.hirepass.com) account.
2. Navigate to the **Check packages** section.
3. Click on **Create a new check package**.
4. Enter a **descriptive name** for your package, such as “MyCo new hire screening”. Then provide a **brief description** of what the package does, for example, “This package runs all necessary checks for new hires”.
5. **Select the elements** you want to include in the package.
